Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You'll need to provide your name, email, password, and select your cooking experience level.
How do I reset my password?
If you forget your password, click "Log In" and then "Forgot Password?". Follow the instructions to receive a password reset link via email.
How can I update my profile information?
You can update your profile information (e.g., profile picture, dietary preferences) by navigating to your "Profile Settings" once logged in.

Live Kitchens

How do I start my own live kitchen?
Go to the "Live Kitchens" page and click the "Start Live Kitchen" button. You'll set up your kitchen name, choose a recipe, and invite participants.
How do I join an existing live kitchen?
You can browse available live kitchens on the "Live Kitchens" page and click "Join Kitchen". If you have a specific room code, you can enter it to join.
What equipment do I need for live sessions?
You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (e.g., smartphone, tablet, laptop) and a stable internet connection. A good lighting setup is also recommended for better visibility.

AI Assistant

What can the AI Chef Assistant do?
The AI Chef Assistant can provide recipe recommendations, ingredient substitutions, cooking timing guidance, temperature advice, technique tutorials, shopping lists, and meal planning suggestions.
How accurate is the AI Assistant's advice?
The AI Assistant is powered by advanced AI models and strives for accuracy. However, it provides general guidance. Always use your best judgment and prioritize safety in the kitchen.

Recipes & Sharing

How do I upload my own recipe?
On the "Community" page, look for the "Share Your Recipe" button or a similar option to create a new post. You can then add your recipe details, ingredients, instructions, and photos.
Can I save recipes for later?
Yes, you can save any recipe you find on Recipe Room to your personal collection. Look for a "Save" or "Add to Favorites" button on each recipe card or detail page.

Community Features

How do I participate in cooking challenges?
Visit the "Community" page and navigate to the "Challenges" tab. You'll find ongoing challenges with themes, rules, and prizes. Click "Join Challenge" to participate.
What is the Leaderboard?
The Leaderboard showcases top chefs in the Recipe Room community based on their activity, recipes shared, live session participation, and community engagement. It's a fun way to see who's cooking up a storm!

Troubleshooting

I'm experiencing technical issues. What should I do?
First, try refreshing the page or restarting your browser/app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. If you still need help, please contact our support team using the form below or via email.
My video/audio isn't working in a live kitchen.
Ensure your browser has permission to access your camera and microphone. Check your device's privacy settings. Try disconnecting and rejoining the session. If problems continue, consult our technical support.
