1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account with Recipe Room, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Cooking experience level
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Location (for localized content)

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our service:

  • Recipes viewed, saved, and cooked
  • Live kitchen participation
  • Chat messages and interactions
  • Device information and IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent

Content You Create

  • Recipe uploads and modifications
  • Photos and videos of your cooking
  • Community posts and comments
  • Reviews and ratings

Live Session Data

When you participate in live cooking sessions, we may record video and audio for quality assurance and to help other users learn. You can opt out of recording at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Our Service: Create and manage your account, enable live cooking sessions, and deliver personalized recipe recommendations
  • AI Assistant: Train and improve our AI chef assistant to provide better cooking guidance and suggestions
  • Community Features: Connect you with other users, enable sharing, and moderate content
  • Communication: Send important updates, cooking tips, and promotional content (with your consent)
  • Safety & Security: Detect fraud, abuse, and ensure platform safety
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our platform to improve features
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and resolve disputes

AI Processing

Your cooking preferences, recipe interactions, and optional feedback help train our AI assistant to provide better recommendations. This data is anonymized and aggregated for machine learning purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information in the following circumstances:

With Other Users:

  • Your public profile information (name, photo, cooking level)
  • Recipes, photos, and content you choose to share publicly
  • Participation in live cooking sessions

Service Providers:

  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics and performance monitoring services
  • Email and communication services
  • Payment processors (for premium features)

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

Business Transfers:
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard cloud security practices
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain necessary data

Report Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to reciperoom@gmail.com. We take all reports seriously and will respond promptly.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site performance
  • Provide personalized content

Types of Cookies:

  • Essential: Required for basic functionality
  • Functional: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics: Help us improve our service
  • Marketing: Deliver relevant content (with consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw Consent: Remove consent for optional data processing

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at reciperoom@gmail.com or use the settings in your account dashboard. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Recipe Room is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will delete any such information promptly.

For users aged 13-17, we recommend parental supervision when using live cooking features and community interactions.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Usage Logs: 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Community Content: Until removed by you or for moderation
  • Legal Requirements: As required by applicable law

After deletion, some information may remain in backups for up to 30 days before permanent removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll notify you via email or platform notification
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised

Significant changes will be communicated at least 30 days in advance. Your continued use of Recipe Room constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
