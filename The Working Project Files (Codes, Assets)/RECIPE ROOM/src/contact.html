Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a live cooking session?
To create a live cooking session, navigate to the "Live Kitchens" page and click on the "Start Live Kitchen" button. You'll be prompted to enter details like kitchen name, recipe category, and maximum participants. Once created, you'll get a room code to share with friends.
Is the AI Chef Assistant free to use?
Yes, the basic features of the AI Chef Assistant are completely free for all registered users. We may introduce premium AI features in the future, but core assistance will remain free.
Can I share my own recipes on Recipe Room?
Absolutely! We encourage users to share their culinary creations. Go to the "Community" page and use the "Share Your Recipe" option to upload your recipes, photos, and cooking tips.
What kind of recipes can I find on the platform?
Recipe Room features a diverse range of recipes, from quick meals and desserts to traditional Sri Lankan dishes and international cuisines. You can use the browse and filter options to find exactly what you're looking for.
How do I report inappropriate content or behavior?
We take community safety seriously. If you encounter any inappropriate content or behavior, please use the "Report" function available on posts and in live sessions, or contact our support team directly via the form above or email.
