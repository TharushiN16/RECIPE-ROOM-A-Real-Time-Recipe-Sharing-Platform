About Recipe Room

RECIPE ROOM: A Real-Time Recipe Sharing Platform


Connecting food lovers worldwide through real-time cooking experiences, AI-powered assistance, and a vibrant community of culinary enthusiasts.

Our Mission

Recipe Room is an innovative real-time cooking platform that connects people through shared culinary experiences. The platform allows users to create virtual cooking rooms where multiple participants can cook the same recipe simultaneously, share progress, communicate in realtime, and receive intelligent AI cooking assistance.


The main idea behind Recipe Room is to transform cooking from a solitary activity into a social, interactive experience. Users can join cooking sessions with friends, family, or cooking enthusiasts from around the world, making cooking more engaging, educational, and fun.

Our Story

Current cooking and recipe sharing methods face several critical limitations that hinder effective culinary learning and social connection:

1. Cooking Isolation: Many people cook alone and lack immediate guidance when facing cooking challenges or questions
2. Static Recipe Resources: Traditional recipe websites provide no real-time support or interactive assistance during cooking
3. Limited Social Cooking Opportunities: Difficulty coordinating cooking activities with friends and family, especially when geographically separated
4. Lack of Immediate Expert Help: No instant access to cooking advice, ingredient substitutions, or troubleshooting assistance
5. Poor Learning Experience: Limited opportunities to learn from others' cooking experiences and share knowledge in real-time.


So in August, 2025 I Founded and Developed a real-time cooking platform with AI integration that facilitates collaborative cooking experiences and provides intelligent culinary assistance.
